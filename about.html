About Us

Ambassador Transportation was born from a dream shared by two lifelong friends who grew up watching their small town's most meaningful moments unfold—weddings, reunions, and celebrations—all tied together by the arrival of a single, elegant limousine.

Years later, one of those friends, inspired by their late grandfather's wisdom—“A journey is not about the distance, but how you feel along the way”—decided to create a transportation service that embodied that sentiment.

Ambassador Transportation has since grown into a service defined by trust, elegance, and care. Every journey we provide is rooted in the idea that every person deserves to feel valued, cherished, and special.
